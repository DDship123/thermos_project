About Us

Zeniva is a brand specializing in providing high-end thermos products, bringing convenient and fashionable solutions for modern life. With the motto "Drink clean, live quality.", Zeniva not only creates quality thermos bottles, but also a companion on all your journeys - from the office, the gym, to long trips.

Our Vision

Zeniva not only produces thermos bottles, but also positions itself as a brand “Wellness Tech” – where people are cared for every day through compact but functional, green and smart products.

Zeniva's orientation:

  • Smart and sustainable products
  • Technology application
  • Originated from Vietnam, aiming globally
  • Associated with health, modern lifestyle, environmental protection

Our Mission

  • Providing products that help users live healthier, more balanced and modern lives every day.
  • Combining minimalist design, smart technology and environmentally friendly materials in each product
  • Promoting Vietnamese values ​​to the world through quality, safe and unique products.
  • Creating a product ecosystem "Zen Living" - where each item serves the goal of green and smart living.

Why Choose US

When choosing a product that accompanies them every day like a thermos, today's customers no longer just stop at eye-catching appearance or competitive price. What they are looking for is trust - trust in absolute safety when using, durability over the years, and quality guaranteed by the strictest standards. Because a thermos is not simply an item, but also a companion - from a warm cup of coffee every morning, to a cool sip of water on a hot summer afternoon, or a quiet source of energy in the journey of taking care of health and maintaining a green lifestyle.
