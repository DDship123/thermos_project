Zeniva Smart Thermos – New Definition of Convenience & Safety

Description about our product:

**LED Touch Screen Temperature Display**

Just touch the lid lightly and you will know the water temperature inside. No more worrying about burning your hands or having to open the lid to check – everything is displayed clearly and accurately with just one touch.

**UV Light Cover – Sterilizes in 60 Seconds**

The UV-C technology integrated in the lid effectively disinfects, eliminating up to 99.9% of harmful bacteria. Perfect for those who value health and hygiene, especially when using drinking water outdoors or reusing it many times.

**Modern Type-C Charger**

Fast and convenient charging with **Type-C** port located at the bottom of the bottle. Just charge once, the bottle can work for up to a week, giving you peace of mind when going to school, work or traveling.

**Optimal Heat Retention – Hot 12 hours, Cold 24 hours**

With a premium **304 stainless steel** shell and **double-layer vacuum insulation** technology, the bottle can keep water hot for up to 12 hours and cold for up to 24 hours. You will always have warm water in the morning or cool water all summer long.

**Minimalist Design – Luxurious – Environmentally Friendly**

Zeniva has a **modern minimalist style** that fits in any space – from the office to the gym. The non-slip, easy-to-clean, and extremely durable surface.

  • **Capacity**: 500ml
  • **Material**: Food grade 304 stainless steel – absolutely safe
  • **Suitable**: Students, office workers, sports enthusiasts, or frequent travelers
